Modify the shape or write a new one, and press the 'Compute Shape' button:

  1. The mouse wheel allows you to zoom in and out.
  2. If you click and drag a node, you can move it.
  3. If you do the same without selecting a node, you will move the entire figure.
  4. Click selects a node, allowing you to view and modify its information.
  5. When a node is selected, the mouse wheel will change its radius.

Button Descriptions

  • Compute Shape: Calculates the shape based on the input.
  • Export Shape: Exports the shape to a text file.
  • Upload Shape: Import a shape from a text file.
  • Preview: Shows a preview of the shape (without the black lines and circles).
  • New circle in the middle of a segment: Clicking on two circles adds a circle in the segment that connects them.
  • Create a circle: Clicking adds a circle.
  • Erase a circle: Clicking removes a circle.
  • New circle connection: Clicking on two circles connects them.
  • Remove circle connection: Clicking on two circles removes the connection.
  • Generate random SVG: Generates a random SVG shape.
  • Generate similar SVG: Generates a similar SVG shape.
