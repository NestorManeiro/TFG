Modify the shape or write a new one, and press the 'Compute Shape' button:

  1. Mouse wheel, while hovering over the SVG, will zoom in (only within the SVG, not the div).
  2. If you click and hold a node, you can drag it.
  3. While holding a node and using the mouse wheel, you can resize it.
  4. While holding a node and pressing Shift, you can adjust the smoothing.
